ABOUT US

Weekly Meetings

We meet every Friday 6-8 pm at the Social Sciences and Humanities Building, room 80. Stop by and play a few games! 

Learning Environment

Chess can be enjoyed at all skill levels. Never stop learning. Never stop playing.

Beginner Friendly

New to chess? Our talented and dedicated officers will tutor you and match you with similarly skilled players.

Rotating Game Modes

Regular Bughouse, Sack City, Bullet, Chess960, and Blitz round-robin tournaments.

Tournaments

A free, unrated tournament every quarter! 

Players are split into sections based on skill level and compete for prizes such as chess clocks, chess sets, gift cards, and Chess.com memberships!

Our newest event can be found on our Facebook events page.
